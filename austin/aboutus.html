About Us

Danaha Global Resources Limited (Danaha Ltd) was incorporated in 2018 and based in Port Harcourt Nigeria. Danaha Ltd offer support services to the oil and gas/Energy sector of the Nigeria market.
The company is managed by seasoned industry engineers and administrator who have both local and internal experiences. Our core services are procurement of equipment and parts,equipment rental, field and laboratory services.

Danaha Ltd is also backed by foreign and local partners who bring together world-class expertise to support our client operations and projects.
We are the exclusive representatives of LEUTERT (Friedrich Leutert Gmbh), selling, renting and servicing of leutert range of sampling and PVT tools.

We also sell and services the MP Filtri range of contamination monitoring tool (Laser particle counters) and also the MP Filtri range of filtration products.
The people at Danaha ltd are problem solvers with specialist knowledge.
These are the people that our clients can rely on to tackle any challenge in the most responsible way.
